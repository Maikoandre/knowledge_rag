# 👑 Severance Crowned (断冕者)

> “Where it walks, lineage is erased, destiny decapitated, and even the stars forget their names.”

The **Severance Crowned** is an **Eclipsed Paragon**, an entity that defies the structures of Dao, karma, and causality by embodying **absolute negation**.

Feared across the [[Eight Desolates]] and whispered of in forbidden sects, it is not born from cultivation or divine will — but from **a betrayal so immense, even the Dao refuses to acknowledge it**.

---

## 🕳️ Origin and Nature

- **Species:** Unknown, possibly post-immortal or anti-immortal.
- **Essence Alignment:** Null-Causality / Severed Dao.
- **Crown:** A halo of inverted karma scripts, bound in fractured law.
- **Rumored Genesis:** A True Immortal who **rejected their Mandate** and chose **Oblivion over Ascension**.

---

## 🌑 Role in the [[Oblivion Maw]]

> “It descended in silence, and the maw screamed open.”

- The Severance Crowned emerged during a karmic implosion at the heart of the [[Oblivion Maw]].
- Fought the legendary [[Unmade Lawbearer]], in a battle that **shattered law itself**.
- That conflict is said to have **obliterated several causality threads**, making it **untraceable by Samsara or the Nine Heavens**.
- It left behind only a black crown floating in negative light, which continues to **consume memory**.

---

## 🔪 Signature Abilities: Techniques of Absolute Negation

| Technique | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| 🕸️ Crown of No Inheritance | Nullifies all lineage-based techniques and ancestral blessings. Entire clan Daos can vanish in its presence. |
| 🔪 Severance of Becoming | Prevents karmic advancement; cultivators trapped at their current realm until they forget who they were. |
| 🪞 Mirror of the Broken Now | Reflects a cultivator’s future path — then erases it. They may live, but only as echoes of choices they can no longer make. |
| ⛓️ Chainless Reign | A domain where cause and effect unravel; strikes never land where they are intended, intentions fail before they form. |

---

## 👁️ Appearance

- Wears a fractured black crown that floats behind its head like a dying halo.
- Robes of ash and mirror-glass, reflecting only what **never was**.
- Its eyes bleed ink — not from injury, but from **conceptual contradiction**.

---

## 🕯️ Cult and Heresy

- Revered in parts of the [[Crimson Hollow]] as “The Final Sovereign.”
- Worshipped by **The Unshaped**, a sect that believes existence is a **curse that must be severed**.
- Sightings are accompanied by **local timeline collapse** and spontaneous **karmic erasure** of witnesses.

---

## 🛑 Forbidden Titles

> These are **names that erase themselves** after being spoken:

- **"First to Sever Fate"**
- **"King of Crownless Kings"**
- **"The Last Immortal Who Chose Death"**

All are considered **conceptual weapons**. Merely writing them in jade or gold has caused entire scrolls to rupture in flame.

---

## 🗝️ Notes & Danger Classification

- **Existence Status:** Confirmed in three layers of Desolate Witness Records.
- **Threat Level:** Terminal (⚠️ No known technique counters its severance field).
- **Access Restrictions:** Class A in the [[Halls of Judgment]].
- **Known Survivors of Direct Encounter:** Only one — a blind wanderer who lost their name, cultivation, and soul lineage.
