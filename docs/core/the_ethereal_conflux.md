# 🌌 **The Ethereal Conflux (灵交汇界 - Líng Jiāohuì Jiè)**  
*A shared dream-realm beyond the heavens, where souls gather across realms to parley, duel, and deal.*

---

## 📍 Overview

The **Ethereal Conflux** is a mysterious metaphysical convergence point located **outside the physical cosmos**, yet accessible to powerful cultivators through their **spirit**, **soul projection**, or advanced **dream-walking techniques**. It is not a realm in the traditional sense, but rather a **spiritual resonance plane** anchored by ancient Dao sigils left by Transcendents.

Though intangible to the mortal world, it manifests as a **floating city of starlight**, surrounded by ever-shifting nebulae, suspended at the border between time and thought.

---

## 🔮 Purpose

The Ethereal Conflux serves as a **neutral meeting ground** for cultivators from the **Nine Celestial Veils** and beyond. While physical bodies remain elsewhere, spirits or soul projections can:

- 💬 **Converse** across sects, planes, or timelines  
- ⚔️ **Challenge duels** governed by Conflux law  
- 💱 **Trade rare techniques**, **Dao insights**, and **spiritual treasures**  
- 📜 **Forge soul contracts**, **oaths**, or **alliances**  
- 🕊️ **Resolve grudges without worldly consequences** (though spirit injuries remain)

---

## 🏛️ Realmic Zones

### 🌁 The Mirror Court
- A grand amphitheater made of translucent silver stone, where **debates of Dao**, **sect negotiations**, and **Heavenly philosophy** are exchanged.
- Home to **Watchers**, ancient spectral adjudicators descended from forgotten sects.

### ⚔️ The Battle Ring
- A massive dueling platform floating above a black star.  
- **Duels in spirit form** are permitted here—loss results in spiritual backlash or memory bleed, but not death.  

### 🛍️ The Pavilion of Echoing Threads
- A marketplace for soul traders, technique sellers, and treasure spirits.  
- Items are often bound by **karma threads** and exchanged through **spiritual merit** or **Dao debt**.

### 🌲 The Waking Grove
- A quiet forest of golden leaves where spirits rest, meditate, or record dream-logs.  
- Considered sacred ground—no violence allowed. Said to be seeded by the Autumn Dream Pavilion.

---

## 🔗 Access & Restrictions

- **Entry Method:** Requires mastery of **Soul Projection**, **Dream Traversal**, or usage of a **Conflux Token (汇界令牌)**.
- **Cultivation Requirement:** Minimum: Late Nascent Soul; Ideal: Soul Transformation and above.
- **Duration Limit:** Longer presence causes strain on the body unless the spirit is reinforced by a **Soul Anchor Talisman**.

---

## 🗣️ Known Visitors

- **Ming Canghai**, Sword Sovereign of the Void-Severing Sword Sect: Known to hold court here once per decade.  
- **Jade-Voiced Merchant**, of unknown sect: Trades in "words never spoken"—lost techniques and forbidden arts.  
- **Black Feather Warden**, of the Nine Shadows: Rumored to recruit spies and assassins through mirror-duels.

---

## 🧩 Legends & Secrets

- Some believe the Ethereal Conflux was born from a **Dao fragment of the True Immortal Realm**, severed in a cosmic war.  
- The **Folded Mirror Gardens** of Hollow Edge Sect may be **a terrestrial reflection** of the Conflux's structure.  
- A **hidden vault** deep in the Conflux stores **unfinished Daos**, **sealed names**, and **unclaimed celestial mandates**.
