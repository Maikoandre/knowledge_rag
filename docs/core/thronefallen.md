# 👑 Thronefallen (堕座者 • Duò Zuò Zhě)

> *“Before the Nine Heavens were shaped, before the Dao split into fragments—there were Thrones. Now broken. Now watching.”*

---

## 🕯️ Overview

The **Thronefallen** are entities—or echoes—who once sat upon cosmic thrones beyond the reach of Dao, Karma, or Samsara. These beings were **not gods**, **not immortals**, but something far older: **Primordial Sovereigns of Conceptual Authority**.

They did not ascend.  
They were enthroned.  
And they did not die.  
They fell.

## 🪐 Characteristics

- **No Fixed Form**: The Thronefallen appear differently to each witness—some as weeping kings, others as black suns or crumbling palaces of thought.  
- **Exist Outside Samsara**: They cannot reincarnate, nor can they be summoned by ordinary Daoist means.  
- **Voice of Command**: Even in ruin, their words ripple across realms, altering fate paths, inciting karmic warps, and infecting Dao comprehension.  
- **Throne Remnants**: Some ruins in the [[Eight Desolates]] or deep void realms are rumored to be their shattered seats.

---

## 🕳️ Origin Theory

> “They ruled before ruling was known.  
> They fell not by rebellion, but by stagnation.  
> The first Dao was born in their corpse-light.”

The **Thronefallen** are thought to be **pre-Dao constructs**, sovereigns of fundamental concepts that predate language. They may have authored early reality, only to collapse beneath their own weight.

Some say the **[[Dao Collapse]]** was not an accident—but a **deliberate dethronement**.

---

## ⚔️ Known Traits and Powers

- **Crown of Fractured Law**: Any technique cast in their presence risks backlash from unknown principles  
- **Grave Sovereignty**: Their presence bends the will of even [[True Immortals of the Nine Heavens]]  
- **Word of Undoing**: Their speech can disassemble formations, sever karmic ties, or unmake Dao Realms  
- **Entropic Halo**: Time, memory, and meaning erode around them like incense in rain
