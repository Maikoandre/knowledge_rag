# 🖤 Void Heart (虚心)

> “Not all things were created. Some were left behind — untouched, unclaimed, unborn. The Void Heart beats in silence.”  
> — Fragment from the Abyssal Codex, inked in unlight

---

## 📖 Overview

The **Void Heart (虚心)** is an ancient, pre-creation artifact — or perhaps an entity — believed to be the **origin and anchor** of the Void Dao itself. It did not come into being through celestial forging, divine will, or Dao convergence. Rather, it **remained** when everything else was born.

Scholars do not agree on whether the Void Heart is:

- A primordial core of anti-creation,
- The beating nexus of unbeing,
- Or the condensed will of the **Void itself**.

---

## 🌌 Location

- Said to be hidden beneath the **Oblivion Maw**, deep within the **Eighth Desolate Domain**.
- Some legends claim it once drifted between the **cracks of the Dao Collapse**, falling into the unformed depths beyond karma and time.

> ❗ It cannot be sought. It can only be found when one ceases to be found themselves.

---

## 🕳️ Nature

- **Essence**: Anti-Dao — it negates all cultivated paths upon contact unless one is aligned with the Void.
- **Energy Type**: Pure Null Essence (零本精气) — repels qi, severs karma, and devours causality.
- **Effect on Reality**:
  - Time slows or inverts nearby.
  - Space folds inward and collapses into itself.
  - Memory, identity, and soul signatures become unstable.

---

## 🩸 Effects on Cultivation

### For Void Cultivators:
- Acts as a **perfect Dao Core**, vastly accelerating Void comprehension.
- Can serve as a **breakthrough catalyst** to become a **Voidborn Ascendant**.
- Grants access to forbidden Void techniques such as:
  - **Oblivion Vein Sutra**
  - **Silent World Devouring Field**
  - **Heartless Origin Pulse**

### For Non-Void Cultivators:
- Induces soul fragmentation, karmic bleed, or spontaneous dao-collapse.
- One’s entire cultivation path may be rewritten as if it never existed.

---

## 🧠 Mythology & Appearances

- 🌑 **Before Samsara**: The Void Heart was rumored to “pulse” at the center of what would become the Ancient Desolate — a singular pulse that "unwrote" three heavens.
- 🪦 **During the Dao Collapse**: It appeared briefly above the Primordial Spire, coinciding with the deaths of several Heavenbearer Saints.
- 🕳️ **Voidborn Ascendant**: Believed to have merged with or absorbed the Void Heart during its final appearance near the Eighth Heaven’s Veil.

---

## ☯ Symbolism

- Represents **freedom from karma**, **rejection of identity**, and **transcendence through erasure**.
- Worshipped by certain radical sects and **Void Dao cults** as the true heart of the universe — a heart that beats silence into all things.
