# ⚖️ Halls of Judgment (审命殿 • Shěn Mìng Diàn)

> *“No karma is ever lost. No sin ever forgotten. In the end, all pass through the Halls.”*

---

## 🏛️ Overview

The **Halls of Judgment** are the cosmic courts where the souls of the dead are weighed, judged, and sentenced according to the laws of the **Heavenly Dao**. Located between **Samsara** and the **Nine Heavens**, they are both tribunal and purification, balancing the universal **karmic ledger**.

Though often associated with divine bureaucracy, the Halls transcend mere metaphysics — they are **semi-sentient institutions**, eternally impartial, vast beyond measure, and older than the first Immortal sects.

---

## 📍 Location

- Positioned at the nexus of **Samsara**, **Karma**, and **Daoic Law**.
- Can only be reached by souls after death or by cultivators through karmic resonance, tribulation, or divine summons.
- Exists partially in all realms but fully in none — its architecture shifts with the nature of its judgments.

---

## 🕊️ Purpose

| Function | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Karmic Judgment** | Weighs each soul's accumulated karma — both good and ill. |
| **Cycle Assignment** | Determines the next cycle of rebirth, punishment, or ascension. |
| **Memory Sealing** | Erases or seals memories before reincarnation. |
| **Divine Sentencing** | Passes judgment on divine or immortal beings who violate Daoic law. |
| **Karmic Enforcement** | Dispatches celestial emissaries or talismans to correct imbalances. |

---

## 🧑‍⚖️ The Judges

The **Ten Judges of Hell** preside over different aspects of karma and existence:

1. **Judge of Origin** – Judges the karma of one's lineage and birth.
2. **Judge of Intention** – Weighs the purity or malice of intent.
3. **Judge of Consequence** – Tallies the ripple effects of one’s actions.
4. **Judge of Silence** – Judges actions never seen, spoken of, or remembered.
5. **Judge of Merit** – Rewards virtue, valor, and sacrifice.
6. **Judge of Betrayal** – Condemns treachery, oath-breaking, and karmic severance.
7. **Judge of Fate** – Aligns or denies fate-threads toward destiny.
8. **Judge of Will** – Measures resistance to temptation and internal struggle.
9. **Judge of Death** – Delivers death truths and severance rites.
10. **Judge of Dharma** – Final arbiter; determines rebirth, elevation, or obliteration.

> 🔸 Some sects believe these Judges are former True Immortals who were absorbed into the Dao after death.

## 📚 Halls Within the Halls

| Inner Hall | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Mirror Hall of Karma (业镜堂)** | Souls are shown the full consequences of their deeds — in past lives and present. |
| **Hall of Weighing Hearts (衡心殿)** | An ethereal scale tips based on guilt, regret, compassion, and righteousness. |
| **Hall of Endless Regret (无悔殿)** | Where particularly cursed souls must walk lifetimes of their victims before judgment. |
| **Severance Hall (断忆殿)** | Final chamber where identities, memories, and names are stripped before rebirth. |
| **Thousand Tribulation Corridor** | Optional for cultivators seeking purification or karmic debt repayment. |

---

## 🌌 Outcomes of Judgment

| Verdict | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| **Karmic Balance** | Soul proceeds into **Samsara** for reincarnation. |
| **Heavy Sin** | Cast into lower realms or tormented before rebirth. |
| **Heavenly Merit** | Elevated to **Celestial Realms** or reincarnated with blessed fate. |
| **Karmic Severance** | Soul obliterated, cast into the **Oblivion Maw**, or turned into a Wandering Wraith. |
| **Judgment Refusal** | Rare souls who defy the Halls through forbidden arts or external pacts are exiled into the **Eight Desolates**. |

---

## ⛩️ Relation to Other Concepts

- **Samsara**: The Halls govern who re-enters and how.
- **Karma**: Their very foundation; karma is the evidence, and judgment is the sentence.
- **Heavenly Dao**: The source of their authority.
- **True Immortality**: Often requires bypassing or defeating karmic judgment altogether.
- **Desolate & Abyssal Cultivators**: Frequently marked for rejection, exile, or destruction by the Halls.
- **Karmic Cultivation Sects**: May send envoys to assist in judgments or retrieve souls of sect kin.
